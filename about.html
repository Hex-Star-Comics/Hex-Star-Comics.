Who We Are

Hex-Star Comics is an independent comic book publisher dedicated to telling original stories that people will love. Founded in 2020 by two people with a dream to tell unique stories that people would love. creators, we hope to tell unique stories that stick with readers long after they stop reading.

Our Goal

Our goal is to build a comic universe where each series stands on its own, yet tips into to a larger story. From what if scenarios to passion filled stories, Hex-Star Comics explores a wide variety of hero comics while keeping character-driven storytelling at its core.

The Hex-Star Universe

The Hex-Star Universe is a shared world where heroes, villains, and anti-heroes collide across multiple series. Events in one comic can lead to consequences in another, rewarding long-time readers while keeping its accessibility to new readers.

Every Hex-Star title is designed to be read on its own or with others - but a bigger picture show itself to those who dive further into the Hex-Star universe.
